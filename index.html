Route A · Online-first growth

For social, e-commerce, promos, and digital recognition

If Beauty Maxx should travel beyond the storefront, Concept 03 deserves serious attention. It may not be the safest traditional chain logo, but it is the most alive online.

Primary: Concept 03 · Gen Z Pop Type
Secondary: Concept 02 · Queen Energy Type
Support: Concept 05 for packaging and profile stability
Best for Reels, sale drops, new arrivals, mobile banners.

Route B · Community beauty identity

For emotional connection, hair identity, and self-expression

If the brand wants to feel close to the customer’s beauty life, Concept 02 carries the strongest symbol: confidence, hair culture, beauty pride, and the feeling of being seen.

Secondary: Concept 03 · Gen Z Pop Type

Best for Wig walls, braiding hair, lashes, confidence-led campaigns.

Route C · Local chain trust

For signage, Google trust, walk-ins, and repeat visits

If the immediate job is local recognition, Concept 05 is the safest balanced choice. It is easy to read, easy to use, and stable across the storefront, profile, bag, and review card.

Primary: Concept 05 · Balanced Badge Type
Secondary: Concept 01 · Popular Friendly Type
Support: Concept 04 for a cleaner chain-style finish
Best for Store sign, GBP, review QR cards, shopping bags.
Feels like Recognizable, local, organized, easy to trust.

Route D · Wholesale / multi-store system

For B2B, distribution, and future brand architecture

If the long-term plan includes multiple stores, salon relationships, wholesale pages, or overseas reach, Concept 06 has the strongest system logic.

Primary: Concept 06 · Expansion System Type
Secondary: Concept 04 · Clean Chain Type
Support: Concept 05 for consumer-facing touchpoints
Best for B2B deck, wholesale landing, labels, boxes.
Feels like Scalable, disciplined, operational, distribution-ready.

Secondary

Use this if the first bet is expansion into wholesale, B2B, and repeatable brand systems.

Strategic note: If Beauty Maxx wants to become more than a local store, Concept 03 is not a risky distraction. It is the sharper online bet. If the first move is local trust, Concept 05 is safer. If the first move is online growth, Concept 03 is stronger.
